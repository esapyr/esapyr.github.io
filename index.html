Hi, I'm Ryan Serazin

I'm an undergraduate student at The Rochester Insitute of Technology majoring in Computer Science, minoring in Philosophy, and expected to graduate May 2016.

My main interests include web development, programming language theory, contenential philosophy, and phase / process music. When I can I program in Clojure, but I sometimes dip into Rust and Haskell for fun.

The project that's currently taking up the most of my free attention is train tunes, an attempt at using Boston MBTA train entry / exit data to make a piece of music in the style of Terry Riley's fantastic In C, with the final goal of streaming data from their live feed.
